## Executive Summary

**Annual Investment:** $120,000 (monthly: $10,000)

## Core Value Proposition

### Problem Statement
Organizations lose decision context over time through:
- Executive turnover (average tenure: 4.6 years)
- Team reorganizations
- Documentation that captures *what* but not *why*
- Dissenting viewpoints smoothed out in final reports
- Forgotten assumptions that invalidate prior decisions

### Financial Impact
This context loss manifests as:
- Repeated strategic mistakes
- Duplicated initiatives discovered late
- Risk misalignment
- Successor paralysis
- Compliance gaps

### ContinuuAI Solution
Preserves decision **reasoning**, not just outcomes:
- Decision rationale with tradeoffs
- Preserved dissent and uncertainty
- Assumption tracking over time
- Pattern visibility (contradictions, drift)
- Continuity across leadership changes

## Risk Mitigation

### What if we don't use it enough?
This is not measured by usage volume. Like insurance or legal counsel, value comes from availability and specific moments of high consequence.

### What if users resist adoption?
90-day pilot identifies cultural fit before full commitment. System designed for executive-level decisions (10-30 per quarter), not daily task tracking.

### What if leadership changes?
This is **exactly** the scenario ContinuuAI protects against. New leadership inherits reasoning, not just outcomes.

### What if it doesn't integrate with our systems?
ContinuuAI is intentionally standalone. Integration is via SSO (authentication) only. No data scraped from Slack, email, or calendars without explicit decision recording.
